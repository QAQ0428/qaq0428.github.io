$\gdef\i{\mathrm{i}}$

复数的平方根

2024年3月23日 21点00分

问题

已知一个复数$a+b\i$, 求$\sqrt{a+b\i}$

过程

$$ \begin{array}{l} \begin{aligned} 设\sqrt{a+b\mathrm{i}} &= x + y\i\qquad(x,y\in\R)\\ 则a + b\i &= (x^2-y^2)+(2xy\i)\end{aligned}\\ 所以\left\{ \begin{array}{l} x^2-y^2 = a&①\\ 2xy = b&② \end{array}\right. \\ ②\Rightarrow y=\frac{b}{2x} \quad\quad\left(x\neq0\right)\\ 代入①得\\ \begin{aligned} x^2-(\frac{b}{2x})^2&=a\\ x^2-\frac{b^2}{4x^2}&=a\\ \frac{4x^4}{4x^2}-\frac{b^2}{4x^2}&=a\\ \frac{4x^4-b^2}{4x^2}&=a\\ 4ax^2&=4x^4-b^2\\ \end{aligned}\\ 令t=x^2, 方程化为\\ \begin{aligned} 4at&=4t^2-b^2\\ -4t^2+4at+b^2&=0\\ t&=\frac{-4a\pm\sqrt{16a^2+16b^2}}{-8}\\ &=\frac{-4a\pm\sqrt{16\left(a^2+b^2\right)}}{-8}\\ &=\frac{-4a\pm4\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}{-8}\\ &=\frac{1}{2}a\pm\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\\ 当\frac{1}{2}a\pm\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{a^2+b^2}&\geq0时\\ a\pm\sqrt{a^2+b^2}&\geq0\\ 显然取负号时是不成立的,所以\\ x&=\pm\sqrt{\frac{1}{2}a+\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}\\ &=\pm\sqrt{\frac{1}{2}\left(a+\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\right)}\\ &=\pm\frac{\sqrt{2a+2\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}}{2}\\ y&=\pm\frac{b}{\sqrt{2a+2\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}}\\ \end{aligned}\\ 以上考虑的是x\neq0的情况.当x=0时\\ \left\{ \begin{array}{l} -y^2=a\Rightarrow y^2=-a \Rightarrow a = 0\\ b=0 \end{array} \right. \end{array} \\所以x=0时, a=b=0,所以\sqrt{a+b\i}=0 $$

总结

$$ \sqrt{a+b\i}=\left\{ \begin{array}{l} \pm\left(\frac{\sqrt{2a+2\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}}{2}+\frac{b}{\sqrt{2a+2\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}}\i\right) &\left(a, b\neq 0\right)\\ 0&\left(a=b=0\right) \end{array} \right.\\​​​ $$
